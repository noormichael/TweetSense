Behold! TweetSense instantly reports on your favorite hot topics: MHacks, Superman, Isis, Ariana Grande, (you get the picture). Find out how people generally feel about these things. A high rating means people associate the topic with really good things like fun and happiness. A rating in the gutter means the topic is displeasing, like violence or sadness. It's all based on our in-house semantic analysis of relevant tweets, presented beautifully in digestible graphs.

Behind The Scenes

We're proudest of our semantic tweet analysis and equally so about locally-weighted linear regression used to predict future trends, all written from scratch.

Our code parses through hundreds of recent user tweets upon request (obtained through Twitter's API) and analyzes each tweet's sentiment. It then displays wonderful line graphs thanks to Google's Chart API.

It's amazing how natural the resulting extrapolations turn out! They reflect true changes of heart! (The numbers don't lie!).

We mined our source data from Twitter's API. We used Heroku for hosting, Python as the main back-end language, while Javascript helped translate numbers into graphs. Of course we used responsive HTML5 and CSS3 for this awesome website. In the future, we hope to integrate Alchemy's API (when we can afford the calls) and Bloomberg's API in the long term to provide even more interesting data.
